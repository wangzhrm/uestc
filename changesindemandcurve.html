A: Upward movement along the demand curve

C: Leftward shift in the demand curve

Result

Movement Along the Curve (Price Change)

  • Occurs when the price of the product itself changes
  • Shows change in quantity demanded
  • Does not shift the entire curve

Shift of the Curve (Non-Price Factors)

  • Occurs due to changes in external factors
  • Shows change in demand at every price level
  • Shifts the entire curve left or right
